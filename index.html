About

Hi! I'm Nicholas Raynes. I am an aspiring developer, with a focus on object oriented programming and web development. The purpose of this website is to showcase some of my pixel art that I have created over the years, as well as to present my ability regarding HTML5, CSS, and Javascript. I believe this project signifies a change in my life, as it represents a transition from artist to developer. I am actively seeking opportunities to practice and learn more regarding software and web development. If you would like to reach out to collaborate or just connect, please contact me through my email, or GitHub profile. Thanks!
